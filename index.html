About Us

Pakistan's Trusted Name in Metal & Aluminium Supply

Dubai Metal Aluminium is a premier aluminium and metal supply company headquartered in Sargodha, Punjab. Founded by Mian Zulfiqar Ahmad, we have grown from a local trader to a nationwide leader in premium metal products, serving the construction, engineering, and manufacturing industries across Pakistan.

Our commitment to quality, fair pricing, and exceptional service has made us the go-to supplier for contractors, builders, architects, and businesses looking for reliable metal solutions. We operate from our state-of-the-art facility at Canal Road, 47 Pull, Sargodha, with distribution networks spanning across the country.
